I'm a nerd. I was born in 2003 and grew up in Sweden where I still live today. Moved out and started working full-time within the AV-industry at the age of 18. I've been programming since I was 14.

I'm passionate about technology. I've always been fascinated by it, like when I realised that it's actually kind of crazy how video AND audio can be transported with an HDMI cable... I am likewise just as starstruck by the wonders and intricacies of *nix systems as well as protocols and specifications that exist around the systems we use everyday.


I have a lot to learn still– in fact, I am never going to stop learning about things. This makes learning resources a significant necessity for me, and I'd like to share some with you, among other links in no particular order.
